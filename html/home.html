Welcome to Bercerita

Bercerita is a non-profit organization that prepares future leaders with the skills to compete globally and work with people from diverse cultures and backgrounds.

Empowering Future Leaders

About Us

We partner with elementary schools in rural parts of the world to facilitate online English communication courses taught by native English-speaking high-school student volunteers. We channel funds to provide facilities, equipment, and technology to schools in rural areas of underdeveloped countries.
