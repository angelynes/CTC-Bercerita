Who are we?

Bercerita, a non-profit, empowers future leaders through partnerships with rural elementary schools worldwide, providing online English courses taught by volunteer high-schoolers and funding technology and infrastructure in underdeveloped areas.

2

Our Vision

To bridge high school student volunteers and rural elementary students by teaching English as a second language with topics that promote and broaden their horizons.
